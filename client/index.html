Instructions

Win by building up an army to destroy the opponent's base

Hotkeys

E - Economy: Spend funds to increase your income. Each additional income costs one more funds to purchase. Income generates more funds every second.

A - Attack: Command your army to attack the opponent. Your opponent will be able to see your army size. If your opponent is also attacking, the armies will fight and kill each other. If the opponent is stealthed, you will damage their base.

S - Stealth: Command your army to hide from the opponent. Your opponent will not see your army size. If your opponent is attacking, your base will take damage.

R - Recruit: Spend 5 funds to increase your army size by 1.
